Open Highlight / Racaf Montage

Total Prize: 3k

Rules

OPEN HIGHLIGHT / RACAF MONTAGE: Rules

  • Event Description:
    • Create a video documenting RACAF from preparation to its conclusion in one of the following styles:
      • POV
      • Vlogging
      • Retro
      • Trailer
    • The video should highlight key moments and showcase the overall event experience.
  • Content Requirements:
    • Theme: Showcase the RACAF journey from start to finish.
    • Duration: 5 to 10 minutes.
    • Format: MP4 or MOV, minimum 720p resolution.
  • Submission:
    • Upload the video to your Google Drive and share the link with the incharges by 23:59 on the last day of RACAF (i.e. 9th Nov, 2024).
  • Judging Criteria:
    • Creativity & Originality (40%): How well does the video showcase the chosen style and unique moments of RACAF?
    • Storytelling (30%): How effectively does the video convey the journey of RACAF from preparation to the final moments?
    • Production Quality (20%): Video clarity, audio quality, and editing skills.
    • Emotional Impact (10%): The emotional or inspirational impact the video has on the audience.
  • Results & Prize:
    • Results will be declared after RACAF.
    • Cash prizes will be given to the winners.
    • The winner’s video will be used in the RACAF 2K25 promo.
  • Disqualification:
    • Plagiarism or inappropriate content will lead to disqualification.
