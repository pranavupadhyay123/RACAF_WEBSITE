Rules and Regulations

Audition

  • Venue: Academic Block, Classroom Number - 02, NERIST
  • Date: 19/10/2024 Time: 12:00 PM
  • For Girls: Neatly tight tops / Black slim fit top, tight jeans / hot pants (Black), heels - minimum 6 cm.
  • Proper hair, makeup, and clothing are required in the audition, which will be arranged by participants.
  • Contestants will be judged on the basis of: Walk, Question and Answer
  • Introduction time will be a minimum of 1 minute.

Talent Round

  • Venue: Academic Block, Classroom Number: 02, NERIST
  • Date: 23/10/2024 Time: 5:00 PM
  • Each contestant will be given 2 minutes for showcasing their talent.
  • On 23 October (Talent Round), dressing and makeup will be managed by contestants as per their requirement.
  • Top 3 talent round contestants will get the chance to showcase their talent on the main day.
